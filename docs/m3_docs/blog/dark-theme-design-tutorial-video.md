# Craft a Dark Theme with Material Design 3

> Source: https://m3.material.io/blog/dark-theme-design-tutorial-video

Posted by
 Material Design
 In this tutorial, learn how to craft a dark theme to complement your app’s Material Design light theme. Learn how to create a dark theme palette and apply it consistently to surfaces, typography, and components. You’ll also learn how to enhance your app experience in the dark with custom color treatments and added touches to express your brand.
 
Want to learn more? Check out the step-by-step [dark theme Design Lab](https://goo.gle/342DuMR) , and Material Design [dark theme guidance](https://material.io/design/color/dark-theme.html#material-theming) .

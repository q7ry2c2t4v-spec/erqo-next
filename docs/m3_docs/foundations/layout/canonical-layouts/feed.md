## Dividing space

A feed composition is flexible enough to allow for content with varying proportions and sizing.

Use size and position to establish relationships among content elements. Feed items should reflow when the amount of available space changes, such as rotating or unfolding a device, or entering a multi-window mode. The order of items is determined by their position. ### Compact
 
A feed layout will stack vertically like a list of cards, with individual items taking up the width of the pane, but not necessarily the full height.
